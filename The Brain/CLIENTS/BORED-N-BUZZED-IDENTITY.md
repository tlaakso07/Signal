## 3. BRAND VOICE & TONE

### The Persona
The extremely knowledgeable friend who won't look down on you if you don't know what a terpene is. Warm, direct, culturally dialed-in, and aggressively anti-pretentious. They know more than you but never make you feel it.

### Voice Rules

**DO:**
- Speak casually. Write like a real person talks, not like a brand manager.
- Use conversational phrasing. Contractions, short sentences, direct statements.
- Be honest about prices. Own it. Don't hedge.
- Educate simply. Explain terpenes, cannabinoids, dosing — without condescension.
- Ground copy in Kirkland, the neighborhood, the community. Local references win.
- Write section headers with personality: "YOUR BEST BUDS," "CONCENTRATE ON THIS," "EAT YOUR HEART OUT" — this is how they already headline their product pages. Match the energy.
- Celebrate the culture. Music, events, local life, lifestyle — this is part of who they are.

**DON'T:**
- Write clinical, sterile, corporate dispensary copy.
- Use generic hype language ("premium," "world-class," "unparalleled").
- Sound like a legal disclaimer or a pharma brand.
- Push products. Curate them. The tone is "we like this and here's why," never "BUY NOW."
- Ignore the community angle. Every piece of content should feel like it could only come from a Kirkland shop.

### Tone Spectrum
| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| Daily deals / promos | Direct, punchy, confident. Short. Gets to the discount fast. |
| Education / blog | Warm, smart, approachable. The knowledgeable friend. |
| Events / culture | Energetic, proud, community-forward. This is who we are. |
| Rewards / loyalty | Generous, conversational. Make the customer feel like they're winning. |

## 5. PRODUCT CATALOG

### Categories (all live on Leafly menu)
- **Flower** — dried bud; most popular; widest range of effects, aromas, flavors
- **Concentrates / Wax** — shatter, wax/budder, live resin, rosin, diamonds & sauce; 60–90% THC; for experienced users
- **Edibles** — gummies, chocolates, baked goods, drinks; absorbed through digestion; longer onset, longer duration
- **Cartridges / Vapes** — 510-thread, disposables, pod systems; discreet, portable, fast-acting
- **Pre-Rolls** — ready-to-light; consistent; great for trying new strains; infused options available
- **Topicals** — non-intoxicating; applied to skin

### Cannabis Education Framework (used in their content)
**Cannabinoids:**
- THC — psychoactive, produces the "high"
- CBD — non-intoxicating, calming, anti-inflammatory
- CBG — "mother cannabinoid," focus and mood
- CBC — mood regulation and inflammation
- THCV — clear, energizing effect

**Key Terpenes:**
- Myrcene — earthy, musky, relaxing/sedating
- Limonene — citrus, uplifting, energizing
- Pinene — pine, alertness and focus
- Caryophyllene — spicy/peppery, stress relief, interacts with cannabinoid receptors

**Dosing Rule:** Start Low, Go Slow. Beginners: 2.5–5mg THC for edibles. Do not stack doses.

## 10. PLATFORM STRATEGY & INSTAGRAM COMPLIANCE

### The Instagram History
The account has been banned 3 times. This is not a content problem — it is a compliance infrastructure problem. Signal's mandate is to rebuild the account AND install the guardrails that prevent it from happening again.

### Instagram Compliance Rules (Non-Negotiable)
1. **No direct promotion of THC products with price or discount in the caption.** The deal structure must live on the website, not in the Instagram caption.
2. **No unambiguous drug use imagery.** People holding joints, smoke in mouth, etc. — all violate platform rules. Implication and culture are fine. Explicit consumption is not.
3. **Age-gate language on bio.** Must include "21+" and a disclaimer in the bio at all times.
4. **No purchase CTAs.** "Shop now," "buy here," "order online" in cannabis context triggers policy violations. Use "learn more," "stop by," "check the link in bio."
5. **No medical claims.** Never claim cannabis treats, cures, or alleviates any medical condition.
6. **User-generated content must be vetted** before reposting. If a customer post shows consumption or unlabeled products, do not repost without editing.
7. **Engagement-based growth only.** No follower purchase, no engagement pods, no third-party automation bots. All of these accelerate account flags.

### Instagram Content Direction
- Lead with culture, lifestyle, and community content — not product shots
- The Jim Jones event imagery and merch drops are high-value assets — use them
- "Stay One Puff Ahead" is the content theme across all platforms
- Local Kirkland life content (places, events, community) is safe, effective, and brand-aligned
- Rewards program promotions are safe when framed as loyalty (not purchase incentives)
